    About Us



    .Located in Mangalpally,Ibrahimpatnam, Hyderabad, CVR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING is endorsed by none other than MHRD/NIRF of the Government of India which ranked CVR among the 101-150 rank group in the country and within top 3 private colleges in Telangana.

    The CVR College of Engineering was established in 2000. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation, India. CVR College of Engineering was affiliated with Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad

    The institute offers a four-year degree Bachelor of Technology, in Nine key disciplines:

    Department of Civil Engineering.

    Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE).

    Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE).

    Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering(EEE).

    Department of Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering (EIE).

    Department of Emerging Technologies (ET).

    Department of Humanities and Sciences (H & S).

    Information Technology (IT).

    Department of Mechanical Engineering (ME)

    Every semester has a minimum of eight courses, which includes a minimum of two laboratory courses. Students are required to attend the institute for duration of not less than four years and not more than eight years to be considered for the award of the degree. The institute follows JNTUH strict attendance regulation, which requires students to put in a minimum class attendance of 75% to progress to the next semester. External (university) exams are held every semester in the campus and consolidated results in all these exams count towards the final aggregate grading.

    The Institute has been consistently ranked highly in major surveys like the NIRF,The Week, Careers360 to name a few. The college is ranked among the top 100 Engineering colleges in the country by NIRF 2020, conducted by MHRD. Based on the Outcome-Based Education (OBE) parameters and criteria, three programmes of the institute have been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA-India) form 2017. The current accreditation is valid until June 2023. The college is also a NAAC accredited. In addition, the Institute has been consistently ranked highly by reputed surveys like Careers360, Career Connect, The Week, Education World to name a few.
